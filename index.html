Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga is a structured, breath-led practice that links movement with a steady sequence of postures. Each position flows into the next through coordinated transitions, creating a continuous rhythm of strength, mobility, balance, and concentration. The repeated sequence offers a clear framework while allowing the practitioner to notice gradual changes in breath, alignment, endurance, and awareness. The practice can be adapted to individual experience and ability, making progress steady rather than forced. Over time, Ashtanga Yoga may support greater overall physical resilience, flexibility, focus, emotional steadiness, and a deeper sense of connection between breath, body, and attention during daily life.

About Me

I discovered Ashtanga yoga over 10 years ago, and it has been my daily companion ever since. Yoga became my safe space, offering me a way to step out of my mind and into my body, and it has been a steady support through my studies, pregnancy, postpartum, and now motherhood. Over the years, I cultivated my practice with dedicated teachers in Italy and Boston, and further refined it through workshops and in-depth retreats. I recently completed a 250 hours training with my teachers Lucia and Roberto at Bottega Yoga in Bologna. I believe Ashtanga yoga is a transformative path and, if practiced at any level, can bring awareness and support to everyday life. ~ This practice is too beautiful not to be shared.
